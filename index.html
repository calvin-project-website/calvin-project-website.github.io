Build your Model

All it takes is a few steps to completly customize your model!

Head over to the admin page. First, name your model. You could be building a model to identify plants or even food! Then, add and name labels. For each label, take upwards to ten pictures to create an accurate model!

Evaulate and Test your Model

Testing your model is just as easy!

Click to the Consumer page. Select the model you want to test. Take a picture, and see your model predict the results. And just like that, you have created, trained, and tested your model. All without a single line of code!
